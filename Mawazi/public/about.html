About Mawazi

Mission

Provide NGOs a simple tool to organize aid fairly and efficiently.

Vision

Timely support for every family with minimal friction and maximum clarity.

How it works

Organizations register, add beneficiaries, create baskets, and log distributions.

Privacy

Each NGO sees only its own data (multi-tenant separation).
